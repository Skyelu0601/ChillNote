Markdown FAQ

Can I export voice notes as .md files?

Yes, ChillNote allows you to export your notes as Markdown (.md) files, making them perfect for users of Obsidian, Logseq, and other Markdown editors.

Does ChillNote work with Obsidian?

Yes. Since ChillNote exports notes in Markdown format, you can easily move your captured thoughts into your Obsidian vault for long-term storage and connection.

Why use Markdown for AI notes?

Markdown is a lightweight, universal format that AI models understand perfectly. By keeping notes in Markdown, you ensure they are readable by any future AI system you might use.
