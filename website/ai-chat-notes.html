The Problem

Information loss in AI conversations.

AI is a brilliant collaborator, but its interface is often fleeting. You might have an incredible breakthrough in a chat thread today, only to struggle finding it a week later.

When you lose these insights, you lose the progress you made with AI.

The Solution

One tap to build your AI memory.

ChillNote focuses on its built-in Gemini chat experience. It helps you selectively keep the most useful parts of an AI conversation and turn them into something you can actually use for your next goal.

  • Note Saver: Turn valuable Gemini responses into notes with minimal friction.
  • Personal Context: Re-feed these notes as context for future Gemini interactions.
  • Better Organization: Keep AI outputs grouped by theme, project, or goal.
  • Powerful Engine: Powered by Gemini for state-of-the-art reasoning and capture.

Gemini AI Chat FAQ

How do I save Gemini chat answers as notes?

ChillNote is designed to make this easy. You can bring valuable Gemini chat text directly into the app and have it cleaned, formatted, and saved as a permanent note.

Which AI model does ChillNote use?

ChillNote is powered by Google's Gemini models, providing a reliable and intelligent foundation for all your voice capture and AI chat needs.

Why keep AI chat history as notes?

Because notes are searchable, connectable, and part of your own personal knowledge graph. Keeping them as notes ensures that the AI's contributions to your thinking remain available for your future work.
