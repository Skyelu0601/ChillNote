FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly is a voice brain dump?

A voice brain dump is the act of speaking all your racing thoughts, tasks, creative ideas, and worries out loud to get them out of your head. It reduces cognitive load and ends overthinking.

How does a brain dump help with ADHD or overthinking?

People with ADHD or anxiety often struggle with working memory and executive dysfunction. The friction of typing makes it hard to record thoughts. Speaking is immediate and creates a tangible record of your thoughts, preventing them from looping in your mind.
