Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Sponteoai ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use the ChillNote mobile application (the "App") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to provide the core functionality of ChillNote.

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Apple or Google, we collect your unique User ID and email address (if provided) to authenticate you and sync your data across devices.
  • User Content (Notes & Text): The text notes you create are stored securely on our servers to enable synchronization between your devices.
  • Audio Data (Ephemeral Processing Only): We collect audio data only when you actively record a voice note.

Important: Your raw audio is processed for transcription and then discarded. We do not store, archive, or retain raw audio recordings on our servers' disk storage.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect generic device information (for example model and OS version) to ensure app compatibility.
  • Usage Data (Local): Analytics events, such as "record_start" and "transcribe_success", are stored locally on your device for debugging purposes. We do not automatically upload detailed behavioral tracking data to third-party advertising platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the App's functionality, including transcription, summarization, and synchronization.
  • Authenticate your identity.
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI services for your specific requests.

No Model Training: We do not use your personal notes or audio recordings to train our proprietary artificial intelligence models.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing & AI Processors

To provide high-quality AI transcription and summarization, we utilize trusted third-party providers.

  • Google Gemini (Google Cloud): We use Google's enterprise-grade AI APIs. Data sent is used solely for processing your specific request and is not used to train foundational public models.

4. Data Retention & Security

  • Zero Audio Retention: Raw audio files are never stored on our disks.
  • Local Temporary Storage: For crash recovery and pending transcription, audio recordings may be stored locally on your device and are deleted after successful processing or within 7 days.
  • Note Retention: Text notes remain stored until you delete them or your account.
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS).

5. Your Rights & Control

  • Delete Account: You can request permanent deletion within the App settings.
  • Access & Correction: You may edit or delete note content at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

7. Contact Information

Contact us at: support@chillnoteai.com
