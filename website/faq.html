FAQ

Frequently asked questions about ChillNote

This page exists for both people and search engines: clear questions, clear answers, and less guesswork before downloading the app.

What is ChillNote?

ChillNote is an iOS AI note-taking app that turns voice recordings into cleaner, more structured notes.

Does ChillNote work on iPhone and iPad?

Yes. ChillNote is built for iPhone and iPad.

Is ChillNote just a speech-to-text app?

No. It starts with speech-to-text, but the goal is more useful notes: less filler, more structure, and easier reuse later.

Who should use ChillNote?

ChillNote is especially helpful for creators, students, founders, busy professionals, and people who get ideas faster than they can type.

Does ChillNote store raw audio forever?

No. Based on the privacy policy, raw audio is processed for transcription and then discarded rather than stored long term on server disks.

Can ChillNote help organize messy thoughts?

Yes. That is one of the main reasons it exists. ChillNote is meant to help you capture first and organize second.

Can I use ChillNote for journaling?

Yes. It is useful for voice journaling, reflection, and quick personal check-ins when typing feels like too much friction.

Where can I download ChillNote?

You can download ChillNote on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.
