FAQ

Answers that help new visitors trust the product quickly.

ChillNote is simple to explain once people understand the problem it solves: saving spoken thoughts without leaving you with a pile of messy recordings.

What is ChillNote?

ChillNote is an iOS AI voice notes app that turns spoken thoughts into clearer, more structured notes you can return to later.

Does ChillNote work on iPhone and iPad?

Yes. ChillNote is designed for iPhone and iPad.

Is ChillNote just speech-to-text?

No. It starts with speech, but the goal is better notes: less filler, more structure, and something more useful than a raw transcript.

Who should use ChillNote?

It is especially helpful for creators, students, founders, busy professionals, and anyone who gets ideas faster than they can type.

Does ChillNote keep raw audio forever?

No. Based on the privacy policy, raw audio is processed for transcription and then discarded rather than stored long term on server disks.

Can ChillNote help organize messy thoughts?

Yes. That is one of the main reasons it exists. ChillNote helps you capture first and clean up later.

Can I use ChillNote for journaling?

Yes. It works well for voice journaling, reflection, and quick personal check-ins when typing feels like too much friction.

Where can I download ChillNote?

You can download ChillNote on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.

Still deciding?

The product makes sense fastest when you try it during a real moment of capture.

Use it the next time an idea appears while you are moving, after a meeting, or when typing feels slower than thinking.
