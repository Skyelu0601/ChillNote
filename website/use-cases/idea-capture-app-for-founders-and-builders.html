The Problem

Your best product thoughts often arrive during walks, commutes, or right between other tasks.
By the time you open your docs or project tools, the original idea has already faded or split into pieces.
Typing everything out feels too slow when you are trying to keep up with a fast chain of thoughts.
Ideas, decisions, and next steps often get mixed together, which makes them harder to ship later.
You need quick capture, but also something structured enough to revisit with your team or by yourself tomorrow.
