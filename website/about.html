About Us

ChillNote is built to help AI understand a real person, not just store more notes.

We think the future does not need more disconnected note apps. It needs a better personal context layer that turns everyday thoughts, voice, and lived experience into something AI can actually understand and reuse.

What We Believe

Today's AI is powerful, but it still does not really know you.

People keep repeating the same context to every model, every new chat, and every new tool. Background, goals, preferences, half-formed ideas, and personal patterns get lost over and over again.

We believe this is one of the biggest gaps in AI today. The problem is not only model quality. The problem is that AI rarely has a durable understanding of the specific person using it.

What ChillNote Does

Voice in. Cleaner structure out. Personal context that can travel.

ChillNote starts with voice because voice is often the fastest, lowest-friction way to capture what is actually in your head. Then AI helps transcribe, clean up, and structure that raw speech into notes you can reuse.

Over time, those notes become more than isolated entries. They become a portable layer of personal context made from your ideas, decisions, preferences, and thinking patterns.

Why Markdown

Markdown is not just convenient. It makes your notes usable by AI.

We care about Markdown because it keeps your notes readable, exportable, and portable. Your ideas are not supposed to stay locked inside one app or one model.

When notes stay clean and portable, they can move into Obsidian, future AI workflows, and any system that needs structured personal context.

Long-Term Vision

Not just a voice notes app. A personal context layer for the AI era.

ChillNote is built for creators, journalers, Markdown-first users, and people who think faster than they type. But the bigger goal is broader: help any AI understand you faster and more accurately.

We want the notes you keep today to become the context that makes tomorrow's AI feel more personal, more useful, and less forgetful.
