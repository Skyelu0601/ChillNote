Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about ChillNote.

What makes ChillNote different from regular voice memo apps?

ChillNote is not just for storing audio. It helps you capture ideas by voice, clean up raw speech with AI, and turn the result into reusable notes instead of leaving thoughts trapped in recordings.

Can ChillNote export notes to Markdown or work with Obsidian?

Yes. ChillNote is built around a Markdown-friendly workflow, so your notes are easier to export, reuse, and bring into Obsidian or any AI-ready knowledge base.

Is ChillNote good for capturing ideas when I cannot type?

Yes. ChillNote is designed for walks, commutes, fast-moving workdays, and other moments when your best ideas show up before you have time to type them.

Can ChillNote help organize messy voice notes automatically?

Yes. ChillNote uses AI to remove filler, preserve your meaning, and shape rough speech into clearer, more structured notes that are easier to use later.

Can I save useful Gemini AI chat answers as notes?

Yes. ChillNote is designed for turning useful Gemini AI chat outputs into notes you can keep, revisit, and reuse later instead of letting good ideas disappear inside history.

What are Chill Skills?

Chill Skills are built-in and customizable AI actions that help you transform notes quickly, such as summarizing, rewriting, translating, or turning rough thoughts into publishable drafts.

Is ChillNote good for journaling without writing?

Yes. ChillNote is useful for voice journaling when writing feels heavy, because you can speak naturally first and organize the entry later.

Who is ChillNote best for?

ChillNote is best for creators, voice journalers, Markdown-first users, and anyone who wants to capture ideas quickly and build personal context that AI can actually use.
