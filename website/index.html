Frequently asked questions

A small FAQ helps both visitors and search engines understand exactly what ChillNote does.

What is ChillNote?

ChillNote is an iOS AI voice notes app. You speak naturally, and the app turns your recording into a cleaner, more structured note.

Is ChillNote a speech-to-text app?

Partly, yes. But it goes further than basic transcription by organizing messy spoken thoughts into notes that are easier to read and reuse.

Who should use ChillNote?

It works especially well for people who think quickly, dislike typing long notes on mobile, or often get ideas while away from a desk.

Where can I download ChillNote?

You can download ChillNote on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.
