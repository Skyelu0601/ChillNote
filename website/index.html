FAQ

Everything new visitors usually want to know before downloading.

Short, clear answers help people trust the app faster and understand where ChillNote fits best.

What is ChillNote?

ChillNote is an iOS AI voice notes app that turns spoken ideas into cleaner, more structured notes you can actually use later.

Is ChillNote just transcription?

No. It starts with speech, but the real goal is better notes, not raw transcripts.

Who is it best for?

Creators, students, founders, and anyone who keeps having useful thoughts faster than they can type them.

Where does it work best?

On iPhone and iPad, especially in the moments when your hands are busy and your head is moving quickly.
