FAQ

Clear answers before you download.

Short, honest, and focused on what most people need to know first.

Is ChillNote just speech-to-text?

No. It starts with transcription, but the bigger value is that ChillNote helps shape your voice note into something easier to read and reuse.

Who should use ChillNote?

It is especially useful for creators, students, founders, and anyone who often gets ideas away from a desk.

Does it work on iPhone and iPad?

Yes. ChillNote is built for iPhone and iPad.

Why not just use voice memos?

Voice memos save audio. ChillNote is built to help you end up with a clear note you can revisit without listening all over again.

Can I use it for journaling and brain dumps?

Yes. That is one of the most natural use cases, especially when typing feels too slow or too heavy.

What makes it feel different?

It is focused. ChillNote is not trying to be a giant workspace. It is trying to help you catch thoughts quickly and keep them usable.
