Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you download.

What makes ChillNote different from regular voice memo apps?

ChillNote is not just for storing audio. It helps you capture ideas by voice, clean up raw speech with AI, and turn the result into reusable notes instead of leaving thoughts trapped in recordings.

Can ChillNote export notes to Markdown or work with Obsidian?

Yes. ChillNote is built around a Markdown-friendly workflow, so your notes are easier to export, reuse, and bring into Obsidian or any AI-ready knowledge base.

Can ChillNote help organize messy voice notes automatically?

Yes. ChillNote uses AI to remove filler, preserve your meaning, and shape rough speech into clearer, more structured notes that are easier to use later.

Who is ChillNote best for?

ChillNote is best for creators, voice journalers, Markdown-first users, and anyone who wants to capture ideas quickly and build personal context that AI can actually use.
