§ 01 — The studio

Save the link.
Steal the pattern.

Every creator's camera roll is full of "saved for later" that never gets watched again. ChillNote turns that pile into a working library — the hooks, the structures, the phrases you already trust.

№ 01 / Capture
01

Video → readable notes in one tap

Save a TikTok, Reel, or Short. ChillNote pulls a clean transcript and lays it out next to the clip so you can actually read what they said.

№ 02 / Dissect
02

Hook, body, transcript — split out for you

Every clip becomes a teardown card: what hooked, what carried, what closed. See why a piece worked.

№ 03 / Capture quick
03

Voice-note the messy version

Talk first. ChillNote transcribes, tags, and files it under the right project later.

№ 04 / Remix

№ 05 / Library

Tag by platform, format, tone, niche. Find the exact hook structure from three months ago in two seconds.

§ 02 — The method

From "I should save that" to your next draft.

i

Capture

Drop a link, a clip, a voice note, or a half-formed sentence. ChillNote files it with a clean transcript.

ii

Break it down

Hook, description, transcript — separated automatically. Now you can see what's reusable.

iii

Run a Skill

Apply your saved AI Skill across the pattern. Out comes the draft of your next post.

§ 03 — Inside the studio

The web app, warmer.

Less "AI dashboard," more "the desk you actually want to sit at." A three-pane workspace: your library on the left, your notes in the middle, your breakdown and Skills on the right.
